Welcome to Resume Maker

Resume Maker has everything you need to create professional looking resumes and cover letters to help you get the job you want.

  • Create professional resumes with Guided Resume — easy, step-by-step guides to help you create sharp-looking resumes every time.
  • Compose dynamic cover letters with Guided Letters— choose from hundreds of pre-written paragraphs created by experts.
  • Fax and e-mail your resume and cover letter together to prospective employers. Requires that fax and e-mail software have been installed on your computer.
  • Post your resume on the Internet — let your resume be viewed by hiring employers at leading corporations and automatically post your resume to every major Resume Bank on the World Wide Web, available to thousands of hiring employers.
  • Find Jobs Online — search for job openings online using Job Finder. Find jobs based on your criteria from millions of openings online.
  • Contact Manager — stay organized, keep track of your job interviews, target specific companies, and follow through with to-do items. View a calendar to keep organized.
  • Virtual Interview and Expert Advice — improve interviewing skills in the Virtual Interview and get Expert Tips from career experts for more effective job hunting techniques.

 We wish you the very best success in your job search!
