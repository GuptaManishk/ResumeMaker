Resume Overview

What is a Resume?

A resume is the front-line attack in any job hunt. It opens doors, which in turn leads to interviews. A well-written resume should put you in a good strategic position for an interview. No single thing is “most important” about a resume, but it must be impressive to do its job. You can use Resume Maker to create impressive, professional resumes. Resume Maker’s Guided Resumes system prompts you to enter the appropriate information for your resume. You no longer have to figure out what to include on your resume and where to place it...Guided Resumes will do the work for you!

Resume Maker also gives you a Guided Resume Window to edit and customize your resume. You can change fonts, styles, and add borders. Here’s your chance to add your own unique style to your resume. Or, if you prefer, you can set Resume Maker to open your favorite word processor.

Hundreds of sample resumes have also been provided to serve as models to adapt to your own needs. To take a look at these, open them from the Guided Resumes section.

The Role of a Resume

Writing your resume with Resume Maker is the most valuable way to begin your job search for the following reasons:

  • You will have a clearer picture of all you have accomplished by putting your experiences and accomplishments on paper.
  • Your résumé's description of your skills and abilities is a confidence booster at the beginning of your job search.
  • A resume is an excellent marketing tool. It will sell you when you can’t do so in person.
