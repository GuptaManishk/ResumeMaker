Guided Letters

Resume Maker’s Guided Letters system helps you create eye-catching, job-landing letters. Create perfect broadcast letters, cover letters, thank you letters, follow-up letters and much more.

Resume Maker’s unique Guided Letters system is a revolutionary concept in professional writing. Resume-writing experts supply paragraphs of appropriate text for your letters and you choose the paragraphs that you want! You can use the paragraphs directly, customizing them with specific information as you are prompted. As an extra bonus 165 sample letters have been provided to serve as models to adapt to your own needs. To look at these, open them from within Resume Maker’s Guided Letters Window.
