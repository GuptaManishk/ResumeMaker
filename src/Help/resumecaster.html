Resume Caster Overview

Resume Maker provides a powerful new tool, Resume Caster, that allows you to manage your resume on the Internet. Resume Caster makes Resume Maker unique as the only resume software that gives you tools to submit, edit, and delete your resume at every major career Web site.

Let your next job find you. Submit your resume to every major career Web site automatically where thousands of employers search for candidates. A unique privacy feature keeps your personal information confidential. At any time you may edit your resume or update your information. Job Search Agents are available in Resume Caster to allow you to submit your name and e-mail address to a career Web site and be automatically notified when jobs that meet your specification are posted to the site. You can also view summary reports that keep you informed of the current status of your resume at each career Web site. Once your job search is successful, you may easily delete your resume from all career bank locations.
